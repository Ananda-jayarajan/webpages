Channa Masala Recipe | Dheena's Kitchen Inspired

A flavorful vegetarian channa masala made with boiled channa, onion, tomato, whole spices, masala powders, coriander leaves, and butter. .

Type Vegetarian
Style Indian Curry
Best With Chapati / Rice

Ingredients

  • Oil - as needed
  • Cinnamon - as needed
  • Clove - as needed
  • Cardamom - as needed
  • Bay leaves - as needed
  • Onion - 1
  • Green chilli - 2
  • Ginger garlic paste - 2 spoons
  • Tomato juice - 1 1/2 tomatoes
  • Salt - as needed
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 spoon
  • Coriander powder - 1 1/2 spoons
  • Chilli powder - 1 spoon
  • Water - as needed
  • Boiled channa - as needed
  • Cumin powder - as needed
  • Coriander leaves - as needed
  • Butter - as needed

Steps

Pan - oil - cinnamon - clove - cardamom - bay leaves - onion (1) - green chilli (2) - ginger garlic paste (2spn) - tomato juiced (1 1/2) - salt - tumeric (1/4spn) - coriander powder (1 1/2 spn) - chilli powder (1spn) - water - coriander leaves - boiled channa ~ boil water - cumin powder - coriander leaves - butter
