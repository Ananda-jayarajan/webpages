Sambar Version 1 | South Indian Sambar

A homestyle South Indian sambar made with toor daal, tamarind water, vegetables, sambar powder, and classic thallipu.

Serves 4 People
Style South Indian
Best With Rice / Idli / Dosa

Ingredients

  • Toor daal - 1/2 cup
  • Water - 2 cups for cooking daal
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  • Oil - little bit for daal cooking
  • Puli / tamarind - soaked in water
  • Oil - for thallipu
  • Mustard seeds - as needed
  • Vendhayam / fenugreek seeds - as needed
  • Urad daal - as needed
  • Onion - 1
  • Green chilli - 2
  • Curry leaves - some
  • Tomato - 1 to 2
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Vegetables - drumstick, eggplant, carrot
  • Hing powder / asafoetida - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Sambar powder - 3 tablespoons
  • Water - 4 cups for sambar
  • Coriander leaves / cilantro - as needed

Steps

✓ Daal: Wash 1/2 cup toor daal and add it to a cooker. Add 2 cups water, 1/4 teaspoon turmeric, and a little bit of oil. Pressure cook for 3-4 whistles. ✓ Puli Water: Soak puli / tamarind in water and keep it ready. ✓ Thallipu: Pan - oil - mustard - vendhayam - urad daal. Add 1 onion, 2 green chillies, and some curry leaves. After sometime, add 1-2 tomatoes. Add 1 teaspoon salt. ✓ Vegetables and Sambar Base: Add all veggies: drumstick, eggplant, and carrot. Add hing powder - 1/2 teaspoon. Add sambar powder - 3 tablespoons. Add 4 cups water. Boil for 10 minutes on low-medium flame. ✓ Finish: Add puli water and boil for 5 minutes. Add cooked daal. Add coriander leaves / cilantro.
