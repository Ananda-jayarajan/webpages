Thakkali Thokku Recipe | Tomato Gravy

A spicy South Indian tomato thokku made with onion, garlic, spices, curry leaves, and coriander leaves.

Type Vegetarian
Style South Indian
Best With Rice / Dosa

Ingredients

  • Oil - as needed
  • Mustard seeds - 1 tsp
  • Fennel seeds - 1 tsp
  • Onion - 2
  • Green chilli - 3
  • Garlic - 10 cloves
  • Chilli powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam masala - 1/2 tsp
  • Coriander powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Tomato - 4
  • Curry leaves - as needed
  • Coriander leaves - as needed

Steps

Oil - mustard (1tsp) - fennel seeds (1tsp) - onion (2) - green chilli (3) - garlic (10cloves) - chilli powder (1tsp) - garam masala (1/2tsp) - Coriander powder (1tsp) - tumeric powder (1/4tsp) - salt (1tsp) ~ low flame ~ 1 min saute - Tomato (4) ~ 10 min saute - curry leaves - coriander leaves ~ oil separates
