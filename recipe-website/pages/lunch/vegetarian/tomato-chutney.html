Tomato Chutney Recipe | Thakkali Chutney

A simple South Indian tomato chutney made with tomato, onion, garlic, red chilli, curry leaves, and thallipu.

Type Vegetarian
Style South Indian
Best With Idli / Dosa

Items Required

  • Tomato 🍅
  • Onion 🧅
  • Red chilli 🌶️
  • Mustard
  • Oil 🛢️
  • Curry leaves
  • Garlic 🧄
  • Salt 🧂
  • Cumin seeds
  • Asafoetida

Procedure

Tomato Paste

  1. Heat a pan and add 2 tablespoons of oil.
  2. Add 5 garlic cloves and 2 red chillies.
  3. Add 1 chopped onion and curry leaves. Sauté well.
  4. Add 1 tomato and sauté.
  5. Add 1 tablespoon salt and a little turmeric powder.
  6. Close with a lid and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Turn off the stove and let the mixture cool down.
  8. Add it to a blender and grind into a fine paste.

Thallipu

  1. Heat a pan and add oil.
  2. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, asafoetida, 1 red chilli, 5 or more garlic cloves, and curry leaves.
  3. Add the tomato paste to this thallipu.
  4. Mix well and turn off the stove.
