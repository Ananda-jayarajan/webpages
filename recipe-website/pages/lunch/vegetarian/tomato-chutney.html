Tomato Chutney Recipe | Thakkali Chutney

A simple South Indian tomato chutney made with tomato, onion, garlic, red chilli, curry leaves, and thallipu.

Type Vegetarian
Style South Indian
Best With Rice / Dosa

Ingredients

  • Tomato 🍅
  • Onion 🧅
  • Red chilli 🌶️
  • Mustard
  • Oil 🛢️
  • Curry leaves
  • Garlic 🧄
  • Salt 🧂
  • Cumin seeds
  • Asafoetida

Steps

✓ Tomato paste: Pan - 2 table spoons of Oil - 5 pieces Garlic - 2 red chilli - 1 chopped onion - Curry leaves ~ saute - 1 Tomato ~ saute - table spoon salt 🧂 - little turmeric - Put lid and let it cook for 5 mins - Stove off - let it cool down - Then add this to blender, make a fine paste. ✓ Thallipu: Pan - Oil - mustard - cumin seeds (Zeeragam) - Hindge Powder (Asafoetida) - 1 red chilli - 5 +> Garlic 🧄 - curry leaves - Add tomato mixture to this Thallipu ~ mix well ~ turn off stove.
