Tomato Chutney Recipe | Thakkali Chutney

A simple South Indian tomato chutney made with tomato, onion, garlic, red chilli, curry leaves, and thallipu.

Type Vegetarian
Style South Indian
Best With Idli / Dosa

Items Required

  • Tomato 🍅
  • Onion 🧅
  • Red chilli 🌶️
  • Mustard
  • Oil 🛢️
  • Curry leaves
  • Garlic 🧄
  • Salt 🧂
  • Cumin seeds
  • Asafoetida

Procedure

Tomato Paste

  1. Pan, 2 table spoons of oil, 5 pieces garlic, 2 red chilli, add 1 chopped onion, curry leaves and saute, add 1 tomato and saute.
  2. Add 1 table spoon salt and little turmeric.
  3. Put lid and let it cook for 5 mins.
  4. Stove off, let it cool down. Then add this to blender and make a fine paste.

Thallipu

  1. Pan, oil, mustard, cumin seeds, hing powder / asafoetida, 1 red chilli, 5+ garlic, and curry leaves.
  2. Add the above tomato mixture to this thallipu, mix well, turn off stove.
