Chicken Soup

A warm spiced chicken soup made with ghee, whole biriyani spices, onion, ginger, garlic, green chilli, cilantro, pepper, and lemon.

Type Non-Vegetarian
Style Indian Soup
Best With Hot / Fresh

Ingredients

  • Chicken - as needed
  • Ghee - as needed
  • Whole spices / biriyani spices - as needed
  • Onion - as needed
  • Ginger - as needed
  • Garlic - as needed
  • Green chilli - as needed
  • Cilantro - as needed
  • Water - as needed
  • Salt - to taste
  • Pepper - to taste
  • Lemon - as needed

Steps

1. Sauté the chicken in ghee with whole spices / biriyani spices until the chicken changes color.
2. Prepare a paste by blending onion, ginger, garlic, green chilli, and cilantro with a little water.
3. Add the prepared paste, along with salt and pepper to taste, and sauté thoroughly.
4. Add water and cook for three to four whistles.
5. Stir in lemon.
6. It is then ready to enjoy.
