Place your chess piece PNGs in this folder.

Required names:

wk.png = white king
wq.png = white queen
wr.png = white rook
wb.png = white bishop
wn.png = white knight
wp.png = white pawn

bk.png = black king
bq.png = black queen
br.png = black rook
bb.png = black bishop
bn.png = black knight
bp.png = black pawn

Recommended: transparent background, same size for all images, 512x512 or 256x256, centered with similar padding.
